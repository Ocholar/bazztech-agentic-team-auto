For mid-sized operations across Africa, this has been a historic barrier to scaling. But AI changes the equation.

## 1. The Market Reality: Constraints as a Catalyst

African businesses often face unique infrastructural challenges: complex cross-border payment reconciliation, fragmented logistics data, and highly varied customer communication channels (where WhatsApp dominates over email). 

Historically, solving these problems required intensive manual human labor. You hired armies of data entry clerks and customer support agents. But as businesses scale, human error scales with it, and payroll begins to eat away at margins.

Enterprises could afford to custom-build APIs and heavy ERP integrations to solve this. MSMEs could not. But today, the barrier to entry for intelligent automation has collapsed.
